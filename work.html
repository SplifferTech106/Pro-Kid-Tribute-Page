Pro Kid

One of the greatest rapper in SA

Linda Mkhize (aka 'Pro formerly known as ProKid) born 25 June 1981,

is a Hip Hop artist from Soweto, South Africa.

He was signed to Gallo Record Company after his independently released track 'Soweto' became popular.

His popularity is largely because he proudly represents South African 'Kasi' (Township) life, rapping in a mixture of vernacular and English.

He believes that rhyming should not be language based but what matters is the delivery, how one can share what they want to share with the people.
